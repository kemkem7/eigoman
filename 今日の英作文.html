Some people say that Ebooks and modern technology will totally replace traditional newspaper and magazines to what extent do you agree or disagree.

Technology is flourishing by leaps and bounds these days. The newspaper which we read everyday has been changing. Once, you get on a train and, look around yourself, you will easily notice that many people are reading the newspapers in gadgets such as , iPad, Smart-phones, laptops. Having said that, there is no reasons that the traditional way of reading newspaper will be unnecessary. There are some benefits on web-based medium. Firstly, most of them are light and portable. With e-books, you can read a lot of books without carrying all of them around. Moreover, e-books render you possible to share the data or a part of the sentences you read with your colleagues or friends. However, there are drawbacks as well. It is not just disadvantages for reading newspapers but digital mediums emit a blue light, which may deteriorate your eyesight.
